We are happy to launch the **CometVisu Blog**! From now on this is the place where we will share:

- **Release announcements** – new versions, highlights and upgrade notes
- **Project news** – roadmap updates, events and community highlights
- **Tips & tricks** – configuration examples, widgets and customization ideas

The blog is bilingual, just like the rest of this website. Use the language switch in the navigation bar to read posts in German or English.

If you have an idea for a blog post – a cool config, a plugin you built, or a story about your smarthome setup – feel free to reach out via [GitHub](https://github.com/CometVisu/CometVisu) or the [KNX User Forum](https://knx-user-forum.de/forum/supportforen/cometvisu).

Stay tuned!
